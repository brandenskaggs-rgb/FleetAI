## What Fleet AI Does

Fleet AI turns vehicle data into **clear, actionable decisions** for fleet operators.

Instead of reacting to roadside failures, the platform is designed to surface insights like:
- *‚ÄúThis fuel pump is likely to fail within the next 48‚Äì72 hours.‚Äù*

## What Fleet AI Is *Not*

- Not just GPS tracking  
- Not just camera monitoring  
- Not another generic fleet dashboard  

Fleet AI treats traditional fleet tools (GPS, cameras, safety) as **supporting layers**, while **preventable maintenance and cost reduction remain the core focus**.
